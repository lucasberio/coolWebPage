My Imaginary Monkey Garden

Edward

Edward is quite mischevious but very friendly. He is a capuchin monkey and

loves to snack on undercooked bluberry pancakes. He hates washing his teeth and

unfortunately does not have many friends because he does too many silly pranks.

Micheal and Jackson

Micheal and Jackson are a silent pair of twins. They are gibbons and all of the

other monkeys are quite scared of them because they talk trash behind the other monkeys'

backs. They do not eat often, but when they do, it is exclusively on taco tuesdays.
